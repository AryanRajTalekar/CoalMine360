Step-1:

Exploration and Surveying

First, we need to find where the coal is. This is called exploration and surveying.
Miners and geologists use advanced technology like drilling and seismic testing
to locate coal deposits underground. Once they find a good spot, they assess
how much coal is there and how deep it is.
