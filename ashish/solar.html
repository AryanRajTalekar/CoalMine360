Solar Energy

What is Solar Energy?

Solar energy is the energy derived from the sun's rays, harnessed through solar panels and converted into usable electricity or heat. It is a renewable resource that helps to reduce reliance on fossil fuels and decreases greenhouse gas emissions.

Benefits of Solar Energy

Solar energy offers numerous benefits, including:

  • Reduction in electricity bills
  • Decreased carbon footprint
  • Renewable and sustainable energy source
  • Low maintenance costs
  • Potential for energy independence

How Solar Energy Works

Solar panels, made up of photovoltaic cells, capture sunlight and convert it into direct current (DC) electricity. This DC electricity is then converted into alternating current (AC) by an inverter, making it suitable for use in homes and businesses. Solar thermal systems, on the other hand, use sunlight to heat water or air directly.
