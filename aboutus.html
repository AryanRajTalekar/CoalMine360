About Us

India faces a complex challenge in balancing its reliance on coal for energy with its climate change commitments. Coal mining is a major source of carbon emissions, contributing significantly to global warming. Our project aims to assist the Indian coal sector in achieving carbon neutrality by quantifying carbon footprints and exploring pathways to offset these emissions. Our web-based application will be an essential tool in this journey, providing detailed insights into emission reduction strategies and their potential impacts.

Objectives:

  • Activity-wise quantification of Carbon emission in Coal Mines.
  • Estimation of existing Carbon Sinks.
  • Gap analysis between Carbon emission and sinks, suggesting pathways to carbon neutrality.
  • Estimation of Per Capita emissions of a Mine.
  • Simulation of different emission reduction strategies.
  • Assessment of clean technologies and afforestation offsets.
  • Estimation of potential Carbon credits.
  • Scalable design for different mine sizes and types.

Future Scope:

  • Integration with real-time data sources for continuous emission monitoring.
  • Expansion to other sectors beyond coal, such as oil and natural gas.
  • Development of a mobile app version for on-the-go monitoring.
  • Incorporation of AI-driven predictive analytics for emission trends.
  • Partnerships with government bodies for widespread adoption.
  • Enhanced visualization tools with 3D modeling of emission scenarios.
  • Collaboration with international coal sectors for global impact.
  • Customizable solutions for different environmental regulations.
